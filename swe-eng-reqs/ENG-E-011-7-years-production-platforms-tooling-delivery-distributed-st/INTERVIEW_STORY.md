# Interview story — ENG-E-011

## Situation
Describe a production DevEx/platform challenge at meaningful scale (multi-team CI, artifact distribution, delivery, or storage). Include constraints: reliability, cost, heterogeneous environments, or consumer blast radius.

## Task
Own technical direction and delivery for a multi-quarter platform/tooling initiative spanning platforms, developer tooling, delivery infrastructure, or distributed storage.

## Action
- Map options and trade-offs (build vs buy, consistency vs latency, centralization vs team autonomy).
- Ship incremental capabilities with measurable adoption.
- Mentored seniors/mids and ran design reviews that raised the quality bar.
- Hardened failure modes via postmortems and continuous improvement.

## Result
State measurable outcomes, for example:
- Build/queue time ↓ X%
- Flake rate ↓ Y%
- Artifact pull p95 ↓ Z ms
- N teams adopted the golden path
- Incident MTTR or toil hours reduced

## Posting phrase to echo (truthfully)
> 7+ years of software engineering experience with building production platforms, developer tooling, delivery infrastructure, or distributed storage systems.
