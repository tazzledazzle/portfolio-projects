# ENG-E-011 — 7+ years production platforms / tooling / delivery / distributed storage

## Best practices

- Map calendar years to **production outcomes** in platforms, developer tooling, delivery infrastructure, or distributed storage—not generic “years of coding.”
- Prepare **2–3 deep case studies** with before/after metrics (queue time, flake rate, deploy frequency, artifact pull latency, cost per build).
- Quantify **scale** honestly: application instances, workflows/day, regions/data centers, team consumer count.
- Connect each case to CoreWeave DevEx surfaces: CI/build, test experience, delivery, artifacts, IDP, or agentic tooling.
- Document **architectural trade-offs** you made and what you would change at 10× scale.
- Keep a living impact timeline so interview stories stay current and truthful.
- Align resume bullets to ATS exact phrases only when the work actually matches.

## Four habits

1. Keep a living impact timeline of production platform/tooling work.
2. Quantify before/after for each case study with a stated measurement method.
3. Rehearse scale and trade-off stories with numbers, not adjectives.
4. Align resume bullets to ATS exact phrases **truthfully**—never invent coverage.
